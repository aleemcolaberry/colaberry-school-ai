# Storyboard & Shot List — Concept 1: "The people who don't know how to build with it"

**Source concept:** [viral-video-concepts.md](viral-video-concepts.md) → Concept 1
**Format:** Cold-open myth-bust / fear-to-empowerment · **Platform:** TikTok + Reels (9:16) · **Length:** 35–40s
**Goal:** Reframe AI fear into urgency → drive Open House registration.
**Talent:** 1 presenter (direct to camera) · **Capture:** face cam + 1 screen-capture clip

---

## Pre-production checklist
- [ ] Cast a presenter who can deliver the hook with conviction (this concept lives or dies on the cold open).
- [ ] Record/clip ~10s of a real agent or Skill running a task end-to-end (for the split-screen).
- [ ] Source/film ~5s B-roll of someone copy-pasting from a chatbot (the "user" side).
- [ ] Brand kit ready: Cherry Red, Leaf Green, Berry Blue overlays + Quicksand/Roboto fonts.
- [ ] Stat cards: `12 weeks`, `5,000+ careers`, `49 countries`.
- [ ] Confirm CTA URL (`learn.colaberry.com` vs. dedicated Open House link).
- [ ] Captions burned in (muted viewing).

## Editing notes
- **Pacing:** the cold open holds (shots 1–2 stay on face), then accelerates through the split-screen + stats. Tension → release.
- **Captions:** every line on-screen; the two hook lines in Cherry Red for emphasis.
- **Sound:** minimal/tense bed under shots 1–3; lift on the stats montage; clean on CTA.
- **The split-screen (shot 3)** is the most production-heavy element — if time-constrained, a quick A/B cut (chatbot clip → agent clip) works as a fallback.
- **Loop seam:** end frame can hard-cut back to "AI is not coming for your job" for replay.

## Variants to A/B (cheap, same footage)
- **Hook swap:** "AI is not coming for your job" vs. "Stop worrying about AI taking your job."
- **Stat emphasis:** lead with `12 weeks` vs. `5,000+ careers`.
- **CTA swap:** "Bring an idea" vs. "No degree. No tests. Just an idea."

## Distribution
- Post native to TikTok + Reels (no watermark cross-post); test LinkedIn 1:1 crop for the working-pro audience.
- Pin a comment with the registration link.
- The hook line ("it's replacing the people who don't know how to build with it") doubles as a strong ad/caption headline.
